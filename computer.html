Welcome to Computer Science and Engineering Department.

Computer Science and Engineering



Computing Facilities:


The department boasts state-of-the-art computing facilities, including high-speed computers powered by Intel Core i7 and i9 processors, running the latest operating systems such as Windows 11, Ubuntu, Red Hat Enterprise Linux, and Fedora. All laboratories are interconnected through a high-speed LAN featuring a dedicated 1Gbps NKN Leased Line connection and 100Mbps private backup leased lines, ensuring seamless and uninterrupted internet access.


Enterprise Servers:


In addition, our infrastructure includes enterprise-grade servers from HP and Dell, equipped with Xeon processors and hot- swappable drives, supporting a wide range of online and offline services.


Software Ecosystem:


To ensure students are well-versed in industry-relevant tools and technologies, we offer access to an extensive suite of software, including:


  • AI & ML Development: TensorFlow, PyTorch, Keras, and MATLAB.
  • Data Analytics & Big Data: Spark, Hadoop, and Tableau.
  • Blockchain Development: Ethereum, Hyperledger Fabric, and Ganache.
  • Cloud Platforms: AWS, Microsoft Azure, and Google Cloud.
  • Containerization & DevOps: Docker, Kubernetes, Git, GitHub, GitLab, and Jenkins.
  • Database Management:, PostgreSQL, Microsoft SQL Server, and MongoDB.
  • Cybersecurity Tools: Wireshark, Metasploit, and Snort.

Library Resources:


Our department library complements this by providing an extensive collection of reference materials, including manuals and specialized books in computer science, AI, and related fields, offering invaluable support for both students and faculty.


Learning Environment:


With an experienced faculty and cutting-edge resources, we strive to create a learning environment that fosters innovation, critical thinking, and problem-solving skills, preparing our students to tackle real-world challenges and contribute meaningfully to the tech-driven world.
